Meet Our Founder

Sbusiso Calvin Mthimunye was born in Siyabuswa kwa phala in the 90th – he started his schooling at Phaghamang, to Sizamokwentu – he completed his high school at Sobantu At tertiary level he studied with the UNISA – community journalism in the faculty of communication science. Mjaphethe video photography was developed and founded in 2007 he learned to take photos, during school trips, he continued to take photos at weddings under the mentorship of Lukas Mthweni.

He loves photography and he hopes to grow the company. His passion is so strong and he intends to make professional photography the thing to be highly valued in his community of South Africa.
He sees photography as his calling even though he didn’t actually study photography. He worked for a local community radio station where his was presenting a show called “Indlukulu – The headquarters” The show is helped him to be famously known. When growing up he used to love dancing. He served as a backup dancer and a singer to a variety of local dance and music groups.
